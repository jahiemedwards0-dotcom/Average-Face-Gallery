About & Methods

This is a static, free-to-host gallery for average faces and morphs. Images live in /images/; entries are stored as in-page data. Avoid essentialist language; include sources where possible.

Disclaimer

Faces here are averaged from real people, then lightly enhanced for clarity and hyperrealism. They illustrate trends in the source sets but are neither definitive nor absolute; as more material is gathered or methods improve, each entry may change. Please read notes and sources on each item.
